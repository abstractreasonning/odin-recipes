Description

Chicken curry from the Indian subcontinent typically features chicken stewed in a tomato-based sauce seasoned with aromatic spices. This recipe, like many others, calls for curry powder (a spice blend made with coriander, turmeric, cumin, and chili powder).

Ingredients

  • curry powder
  • cloves garlic, minced
  • skinless, boneless chicken breast halves

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a skillet over medium heat.
  2. Stir in garlic, curry powder, cinnamon, paprika, bay leaf, ginger, sugar, and salt.
  3. Add chicken pieces, tomato paste, yogurt, and coconut milk. Bring to a boil, reduce heat, and simmer
  4. Remove bay leaf, and stir in lemon juice and cayenne pepper.
